    <h1>The Fog News – Comprehensive Multimedia & E-Paper Platform</h1>

    <div class="section">
        <h2>Project Overview</h2>
        <p>
            The Fog News is a dynamic online news and recruitment platform delivering real-time updates on climate, weather, and share market trends. Users can access e-paper editions, multimedia content, participate in polls and quizzes, and explore job circulars. Companies and media personnel can manage content efficiently via dedicated admin, reporter, and editor panels, making this a robust news and recruitment ecosystem.
        </p>
    </div>

    <div class="section">
        <h2>Key Features</h2>
        <ul>
            <li><strong>E-Paper Integration:</strong> Access daily news editions online.</li>
            <li><strong>Multimedia Support:</strong> Videos, images, and interactive content.</li>
            <li><strong>Real-Time Updates:</strong> Climate, weather, and share market trends for informed decision-making.</li>
            <li><strong>Polls & Quizzes:</strong> Users can vote in polls and participate in quizzes.</li>
            <li><strong>Job Portal:</strong> Companies can post circulars and receive applications directly.</li>
            <li><strong>Automated Notifications:</strong> Applicant details and cover letters are auto-forwarded to company emails.</li>
            <li><strong>Dedicated Panels:</strong>
                <ul>
                    <li><strong>Admin Panel:</strong> Manage users, content, polls, quizzes, and analytics.</li>
                    <li><strong>Reporter Panel:</strong> Submit and manage news articles.</li>
                    <li><strong>Editor Panel:</strong> Review and approve content for publication.</li>
                </ul>
            </li>
            <li><strong>Modern UI/UX:</strong> Clean, intuitive, and fully responsive design ensuring easy navigation and engagement.</li>
        </ul>
    </div>

    <div class="section">
        <h2>Tech Stack</h2>
